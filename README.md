**Notes on Intent**

This repository contains applied Systems Thinking Framework analyses on various AI topics.
These documents are public but are not optimized for visibility.
They are my notes and ongoing commitment to thinking structurally about complex systems.

**WHAT THIS IS**

- Working notes
- Ongoing practive in modeling complex systems

**WHAT THIS IS NOT**

- Forecasts
- Hot takes
- Policy prescriptions

** WHY SYSTEMS THINKING?**

- Objective: to understand the dynamics of complex systems, not to predict specific outcomes. 

**METHOD**

Each analysis generally follows the Systems Thinking Framework structure:

- Linear narrative vs structural reality  
- Problem framing  
- System boundary  
- Stocks & flows  
- Reinforcing loops  
- Balancing loops  
- Delays  
- Leverage points (Donella Meadows)  
- Paradigm layer  
- Role-level or institutional implications 

Insights and keytake aways are highlighted at the end of each analysis.
